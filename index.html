Tokyo Kyoto Mt.Fuji
Tokyo is a dazzling blend of ultra-modern skyscrapers and historic temples. From the bustling streets of Shibuya and the neon lights of Shinjuku to the peaceful gardens of the Imperial Palace, the city offers something for everyone.
Kyoto is Japan’s cultural heart, famous for its ancient temples, serene Zen gardens, and traditional tea houses. Stroll through the enchanting bamboo forest in Arashiyama or catch a glimpse of a geisha in the historic Gion district.
Mount Fuji, Japan’s tallest and most iconic mountain, is a must-see for visitors. Whether you admire its perfect cone from afar or climb to its summit, Fuji’s beauty and spiritual significance make it a symbol of Japan.
